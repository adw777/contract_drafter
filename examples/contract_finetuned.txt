### 1. Services Provided

The Contractor agrees to provide the following services:

- **Legal Consultations:** Offering professional legal advice in areas such as Corporate Law, Intellectual Property Law, Taxation, etc., through phone calls, video conferencing, or in-person meetings at designated locations.
- **Document Preparation:** Drafting legal documents including, but not limited to, Memorandum & Articles of Association, Deeds, Trust Deeds, Power of Attorney, and Agreements with other parties.

---

### 2. Pricing

- Each service consultation starts at **₹10,000/- per hour**.
- Additional hourly rates apply for extended consulting sessions due to complex cases or large volumes of work.

---

### 3. Payment Terms

- Payments will be made upon completion of each service, unless agreed otherwise before commencing the project.
- A fixed sum of **₹50,000/-** will be paid upfront for projects exceeding a certain threshold (to be discussed).
- Ongoing payments will be based on the hours worked; specific rates to be negotiated separately.
- Late payments beyond **30 days** from the due date may be subject to additional fees unless excused in writing.

---

### 4. Confidentiality

All client data provided during consultations will remain confidential unless disclosure is required by law.

---

### 5. Term of Agreement

- This agreement is effective upon signing by both parties.
- Either party may terminate this agreement by providing written notice.

---

### 6. Termination

- Either party may terminate this agreement with proper written notice.

---

### 7. Governing Law

- This contract shall be governed by the **laws of India**.

---

### 8. Dispute Resolution

- Any disputes arising under this contract shall be resolved through **arbitration** as per the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 (India).
- If mediation fails, one neutral arbitrator will resolve the dispute.

---

### 9. Indemnification

- Both parties agree to indemnify each other against third-party claims arising from negligence, breach, or misuse of services provided under this contract.
